Paying back did not pinch me………QUICK MONEY = NEOGROWTH

Like any business, the garment business also requires working capital, there are lot of regular expenses in day to day running of your business like cost for equipment, interiors, and employee salary etc. I am Supper happy to find NeoGrowth to help me meet urgent working capital requirements.

Flexibility while repaying………

Among all good things about NeoGrowth what I like most? Is the flexibility of repayment basis my business performance. I though repayment of loan is always a very stubborn process where bank people will always want a fixed money back on a fixed date irrespective of who my business is performing. Post finding NeoGrowth I was happy to know that I was wrong. As NeoGrowth provide working capital funding with a repayment process which is directly proportional to my business performance. That implies that when I have good value of card swipes at my shop, I re-pay more of my loan amount but if there is slow down in my business, I pay less. This flexibility to repay has given me comfort.

NeoGrowth understands my requirements, their repayment model is superb….by linking repayment only as a promotion to my card swipe business has given me comfort as I get more time to repay my loan if business slows down. In true sense it’s like a business partnership.

I would like to say thanks to the entire NeoGrowth customer relationship team for the superservice and facilitating my funding. I will definitely let everyone I know to use this great service!!!

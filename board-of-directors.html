NeoGrowth Board Of Directors

Thomas Hyland

Partner, Aspada Investment Advisors

  • Tom is a Co-Founder and Partner of Aspada Investment Advisors and joined the board of NeoGrowth in March 2014.

  • Previously, he was one of the first employees of SeaChange Capital Partners, a social sector financial intermediary founded by former senior partners of Goldman Sachs.

  • He also worked at Goldman Sachs in San Francisco for one of largest wealth management teams in the firm.

  • Tom received his MBA at the Indian School of Business (ISB) in Hyderabad, and his BA in Economics from Providence College. While at ISB he focused on innovations in early stage financing and entrepreneurial ecosystems in India and other emerging markets.

  • He has worked and traveled extensively throughout India and Asia.

  • Additionally he serves on the Board of ThinkLink Supply Chain Services.

B. S. Nagesh

Independent Director

  • He is the Founder of TRRAIN (Trust for Retailers and Retail Associates of India).

  • He is also the founder chairman of RAI (Retail Association of India) and continues to champion the cause of Indian retail industry and upgrading the lives of people. He joined the board of NeoGrowth in January 2015.

  • He has played Instrumental role in setting up Hypercity, a chain of hypermarkets. He has received his MBA at Benares Hindu University.

  • He has received his MBA at Benares Hindu University.

  • He is also a board member at various marquee organizations such as Marico Limited, IGDS – A global association of department stores, and ENIL (Radio Mirchi) of Times of India Group.

  • He is a retail industry veteran, who is credited with ushering in international brands and modern retailing in India.

  • He is the only Indian retailer and the first Asian retailer to take a significant place alongside more than 100 achievers in the World Retail Hall of Fame.

Mark Straub

Co-founder, Khosla Impact

  • He has been passionate about the power of market-based models to fight global poverty since he traded investment banking for micro-lending in 2006.He joins the board of NeoGrowth in April 2015.

Ganesh Ramaswamy

Managing Director, Frontier Inverstment Groups

  • He is the Managing Director of Frontier Investments Group and joined the board of NeoGrowth in July 2015.

  • He is seasoned entrepreneur and has been an impact technology venture investor across India, Asia and Silicon Valley.

  • Earlier he was General Partner at Lok Capital, a venture growth fund that invests in social enterprises with innovative and technologically advanced services in financial inclusion, education, and employability.

Prior to Unitus Inc., he led the entry of Greylock Partners, a leading Silicon Valley venture growth fund, into the Indian market.

C. V. Madhukar

Leads Governance and Citizen Engagement investments Omidyar Network India Advisors

  • He was the co-founder and director of PRS legislative Research, an institution that aims to make India's legislative process better informed, more transparent and participatory. He joins the board of NeoGrowth in March 2015.

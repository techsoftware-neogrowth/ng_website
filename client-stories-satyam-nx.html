As a started to need more working capital. I had to look forward for alternatives to raise money. By God’s grace, some on referred NeoGrowth to me.

In my first meeting itself, NeoGrowth team had not only understood my business requirements well but also were extremely responsive to my needs and they could understand that few customer do not have established credit histories, and well documented financial statements, they could provide me business loan primarily only basis card swipe sale at my shop. I am thankful to NeoGrowth, who have helped us not only expand our business! This expansion has enabled us to create several direct and indirect new jobs which make us extremely satisfied for being able to provide so many young people a livelihood.
